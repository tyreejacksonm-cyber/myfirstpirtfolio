Bio

Tyree Shaw is an aspiring UX designer and creative technologist with a passion for gaming, storytelling, and user-centered design. He is currently studying Graphic Information Technology with a focus in UX design at Arizona State University, where he is developing skills in UX research, interface design, and prototyping.

He has also managed the Adobe Admin Console for Better Youth, administering licenses and supporting remote installations. As an advocate for creative tools such as Adobe XD, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Audition, and Character Animator, TJ has helped guide and inspire other creatives—encouraging participation in events like Adobe MAX and sharing insights from those experiences.

My Interests

Im interested in UX research, UI design, and prototyping. I also enjoy working on projects related to gaming, entertainment, and real-world experiences.

Goals

My goal is to build a strong portfolio and work in the gaming or entertainment industry, creating experiences that people enjoy and remember.
